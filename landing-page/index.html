How It Works

1. Capture

Drop raw ideas (text, notes, AI chat exports) into your `ideas/inbox` folder.

2. Orchestrate

Your AI agent automatically detects new ideas and prepares them for processing.

3. Delegate & Analyze

The AI processes the idea, cross-referencing it with your strategic goals.

4. Organize

Structured, enriched content is automatically saved to `ideas/processed`.

5. Version

All changes are committed to Git, providing a complete history and backup.
